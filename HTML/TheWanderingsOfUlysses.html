The Wanderings of Ulysses

About the Section

"The Wanderings of Ulysses", the second section of Ulysses, is comprised of twelve chapters, four of which we analyzed. The four sections we worked with are:

  • Calypso
  • Lotus Eaters
  • Wandering Rocks
  • Cyclops

In these four chapters, we encoded 95 locations, which are included on the left.

Considering the homeric parallel, we can remember that the sections of The Oddysey that most closely correlate with the chapters in "Wanderings" are the ones where Odysseus is adventuring and trying to get back home.
