About this Location

Eccles Street is a street in Dublin, Ireland, on the South side of the Liffey. This website provides a very decent summary of this location, especially in the context of it being a very prominent location of Ulysses. Characters in Ulysses, most notably Blazes Boylan, visit Eccles Street. 7 Eccles street is the home of Molly and Leopold Bloom.
