Research Question

If you've ever encountered Ulysses before, you'll know that it's kind of a monster of a book. It's long. It's hard to read. There's a lot going on with it. It would be impossible to analyze everything that there is to analyze about this book during the course of a semester, so we decided to focus on one aspect of Ulysses: how Joyce recreates the city of Dublin through his novel.
