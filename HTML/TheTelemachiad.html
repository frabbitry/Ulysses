The Telemachiad

About the Section

"The Telemachiad," the first section of Ulysses, includes three chapters, which use three settings, as listed on the side of the page. The three chapters of "The Telemachiad" are

  • Telemachus
  • Nestor
  • Proteus

Considering the homeric parallel, the Telemachiad focuses on Stephen Dedalus and Leopold Bloom, and these first three chapters serve as introductions to the larger narrative arcs of Ulysses. On a simpler level, in "The Telemachiad," the day June 16, 1904, when the book is set, begins.
